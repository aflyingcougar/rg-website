About Us

Our Mission

We are the guys bringing you Random Gamers, a website where you can join us in playing CS:GO competitvely on our servers. We want you to be able to experience quality competitive gaming without having to deal with griefers, smurfs, hackers, and generally unpleasant people. Come join us and be part of a small but dedicated community of CS:GO players. Our servers offer quick pick up gaming, and we monitor and ban players who are not respectful of others' desire to participate in fair, honest competition. We are currently looking for loyal members who are willing to help us build this community.

Our Offerings

We offer monthly drawings for skins to players who have donated at least $2 each month. You will also receive a Donor Badge next to your name when playing on our servers if you donate at least $1 each month. These are pay as you go, so no subscription is necessary. Our servers are pug enabled so that games are easy to access.

Our Needs

We are a small team, completely reliant on donations to maintain our servers. That being said, the cost to keep the servers running will always be posted on the website, and we will show how much money has been donated toward our target for each month. This will be displayed in the upper right hand corner. We are not asking for much, just a dollar each month and we can continue our service to our fellow players. Please note that donations are not mandatory, and we do not require a subscription to access our servers.
